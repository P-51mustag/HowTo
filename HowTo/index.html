Crocheting Stitches for Beginners

Single Crochet

  • Make a line of chains
  • When you have a line of chains flip your work
  • Insert your hook into the second chain from your hook and go through both loops
  • Grab string and pull through
  • Yarn over again and pull through both loops

Double Crochet

  • Start with a line of chains then flip your work
  • Yarn over then insert your hook second chain from hook
  • Grab string and pull through
  • Yarn over pull through two loops
  • Yarn over and pull through remaining loops

Triple Crochet

  • Start with a line of chains then flip your work
  • Yarn over twice then insert your hook second chain from hook
  • Grab string and pull through
  • Yarn over pull through two loops
  • Yarn over pull through two loops
  • Yarn over pull through remaining loops

Half Double Crochet

  • Start with a line of chains then flip your work
  • Yarn over then insert your hook second chain from hook
  • Grab string and pull through
  • Yarn over and pull through all loops

Half Triple Crochet

  • Start with a line of chains then flip your work
  • Yarn over twice then insert your hook second chain from hook
  • Grab string and pull through
  • Yarn over pull through two loops
  • Yarn over pull through remaining loops

Slip Stitch

  • Start with a line of chains
  • Make sure the chain is straight then put the two ends together
  • Insert the hook into the first chain
  • Grab the string and pull through all the way
